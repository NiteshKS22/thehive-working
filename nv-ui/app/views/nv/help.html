Welcome to NeuralVyuha

NeuralVyuha (NV) is the new engine powering your SOC. It is designed to handle massive volumes of alerts and automatically group them into meaningful incidents called Vyuhas.

This help center guides you through the new features and workflows available in the NV dashboard.

Triaging Incidents (Vyuhas)

Instead of chasing individual alerts, you now triage Groups of related alerts.

  1. Navigate to NeuralVyuha Incidents in the sidebar.
  2. Sort by Severity or Confidence to prioritize.
  3. Click on a Group ID to view its details.
  4. Review the Unified Timeline to see the sequence of events.
  5. If the group represents a real threat, convert it to a Case.

Understanding the Unified Timeline

The Timeline combines data from multiple sources into a single view:

  • Alerts: Raw security events.
  • Tasks: Actions taken by analysts.
  • Notes: Collaborative comments.

Events are sorted chronologically, with the newest at the top.

Confidence Scores Explained

Every Correlation Rule assigns a Confidence Score (0-100) to the groups it creates.

High (80-100)
Very likely a true positive. Prioritize these.
Medium (50-79)
Requires investigation. Could be suspicious behavior.
Low (0-49)
Likely noise or informational. Review periodically.
